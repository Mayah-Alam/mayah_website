Plot Web Mobile App

Team size: 4
When: January-April 2024
About: My team and I developed a mobile app in our Mobile App Development for Entrepreneurs class. Our app enables writers to visualize their character relationships in a story. We originally planned on making an app where University Michigan students could check how busy the campus gyms are by equipment section. The school would not give us the data we needed so we had to pivot half way through the semster. We made an MVP 1, conductued user testing, and made an MVP2. We managed a database of characters and relationships between them using SQLite. We also used a visual web to represent data that moved when a user touched it. Personally, I made custom clases in Flutter to ensure visual consistency throughout the app.
